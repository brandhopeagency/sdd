# Domain and Certificate Ownership

## Scope

- `mentalhelp.chat`
- `www.mentalhelp.chat`

## Ownership

- Primary owner: Platform/Infrastructure team
- Backup owner: DevOps on-call
- Escalation channel: `#infra-alerts`

## Responsibilities

- Maintain DNS records and canonical redirect behavior.
- Ensure managed certificates remain active and trusted.
- Review certificate expiry posture weekly and before releases.
- Execute renewal continuity drills before major releases.

## Sign-off Requirements

- Ownership acknowledgment must be captured in `specs/001-configure-domain-environments/evidence/ownership-signoff.md`.
